Treasure Hoarding

Goblins love collecting shiny objects and treasures. They often steal from travelers and hoard their loot in hidden caves or dark forests.

Hunting and Foraging

In many stories, goblins are resourceful hunters and gatherers. They survive by foraging in forests or hunting small animals.
